// Walkable Wrecks

Kill it. Board it. Loot it.

Most space games end an encounter when the explosion fades. We don't. Every NPC ship you destroy in Space Wars 3000 leaves a real, walkable derelict in space.

Dock with the wreck, step into a 2D ship interior on foot, and recover credits, cargo, and components from its decks. The first captain to arrive wins the spoils — so the action never really stops.

  • Manifests are persisted, so a server restart won't wipe your loot.
  • Only combat participants and ships in the same sector can board — no remote scavenging.
  • Atomic loot claiming: no double-awards, no ghost crates.
