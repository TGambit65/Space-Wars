Trading

Trading is the primary way to earn credits in Space Wars 3000. Buy commodities where they're cheap (produced) and sell where they're expensive (consumed).

Port Types

There are 8 types of ports, each with different specializations:

Pricing

Prices are dynamic and fluctuate based on supply and demand:

  • Base price — Each commodity has a base price (shown in the tables above)
  • Price range — Actual prices vary from 0.5× to 2.0× the base price
  • Volatility — Higher volatility means bigger price swings
  • Price spread — The difference between buy and sell prices is 15%
  • Tax — Each port charges a tax rate on transactions (2%–10%)

Profitable Trading

The most profitable trades come from buying at a port that produces a commodity (low price) and selling at a port that consumes it (high price). For example:

  • Buy Ore at a Mining Outpost → Sell at a Tech Center
  • Buy Electronics at a Tech Center → Sell at an Agricultural Station
  • Buy Food at an Agricultural Station → Sell at a Mining Outpost
