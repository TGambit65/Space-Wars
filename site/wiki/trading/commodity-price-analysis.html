📊 Understanding Price Fluctuation Patterns

Prices don't move randomly - they follow patterns based on supply, demand, and player behavior! Learn to recognize these patterns and you'll always know where the market is heading! 🎯

📈 Common Price Movement Patterns

🌊 The Supply Cycle Pattern

How Supply Affects Prices

Supply Cycle Analysis
PHASE 1 Low Supply → High Prices → High Profits
PHASE 2 High Profits → More Traders → More Supply
PHASE 3 High Supply → Lower Prices → Lower Profits
PHASE 4 Low Profits → Traders Leave → Low Supply
CYCLE Pattern repeats every 3-7 days!

💡 Trading Strategy

  • Phase 1: Buy heavily - prices will keep rising!
  • Phase 2: Sell gradually - peak is coming!
  • Phase 3: Avoid this commodity - prices falling!
  • Phase 4: Get ready - next opportunity coming!

Events That Cause Price Changes

🚀 Price Increase Events
  • War Outbreaks: +50-200% weapons/armor

🎯 Event Trading Strategy: Watch the news! Major events create massive opportunities for traders who react quickly! 📰

📊 Technical Analysis Indicators

📈 Trend Analysis

How to Spot Price Trends
  • Uptrend: 3+ consecutive price increases
  • Downtrend: 3+ consecutive price decreases
  • Sideways: Price bouncing in narrow range
  • Breakout: Price suddenly breaks trend
Trend Signals
BUY SIGNAL Price breaks above resistance line
SELL SIGNAL Price breaks below support line

📊 Volume Analysis

Using Trading Volume as a Predictor
  • High Volume + Rising Price: Strong uptrend
  • High Volume + Falling Price: Strong downtrend
  • Low Volume + Any Move: Weak signal
  • Volume Spike: Major move coming!
💡 Volume Secret: Big price moves with low volume usually reverse quickly - don't chase them! 🏃‍♂️

🏭 Supply Chain Analysis

Understanding the Production Chain

Every commodity is part of a supply chain - understanding it gives you prediction power!

Example: Energy Cell Supply Chain
STEP 1 Raw Fuel mined from gas giants
STEP 2 Fuel refined at processing stations
STEP 3 Energy Cells manufactured in factories
STEP 4 Cells distributed to markets
PREDICTION If raw fuel price rises → energy cells will follow!

🎯 Supply Chain Trading Strategy

  2. Predict Processing Delays: 2-3 day lag usually
  3. Buy Before the Wave: Get finished goods early
  4. Sell at Peak: When everyone else catches on

🎯 Price Prediction Formula

🧮 Mathematical Price Prediction Model

The Professional Trader's Formula

Price Prediction Algorithm
BASE CALC Future Price = Base Price × Supply Factor × Demand Factor
SUPPLY Supply Factor = (Average Supply ÷ Current Supply)
DEMAND Demand Factor = (Current Demand ÷ Average Demand)
EVENTS Event Modifier = 1.0 + (Event Impact × Event Probability)
FINAL Predicted Price = Base Calc × Event Modifier × Trend Factor

🎯 Advanced Timing Techniques

🎪 The Carnival Ride Strategy

🎢 Riding the Price Waves

Like a carnival ride, commodity prices go up and down in predictable cycles!

Wave Riding Strategy
TROUGH Price at bottom - BUY MAXIMUM
RISING Price climbing - HOLD & maybe buy more
PEAK Price at top - SELL EVERYTHING
FALLING Price dropping - WAIT for trough

🎯 Wave Identification Tips

  • Trough Signs: Everyone's panicking, volume spiking down
  • Peak Signs: Everyone's excited, volume spiking up
  • Average Cycle: 7-14 days for most commodities
  • Multiple Waves: Some commodities have 2-3 peaks!
⚡ Lightning Strike Trading

🌩️ Capitalizing on Sudden Events

Sometimes opportunities appear and disappear in minutes - be ready!

🚨 Lightning Opportunity Signals
  • News Alert: War declared, peace treaty, etc.
  • Price Spike: >50% move in <1 hour
  • Volume Explosion: 10x normal trading
  • Chat Buzz: Everyone talking about one commodity
⚡ Lightning Trading Rules
  • React Fast: Opportunities last 30-120 minutes
  • Size Small: High risk = smaller positions
  • Set Limits: Pre-plan your buy/sell points
  • Exit Quick: Take profits fast, don't get greedy
⚠️ Lightning Risk: These opportunities can turn into disasters just as fast! Never risk more than 10% of your credits on lightning trades! ⚡

📏 Position Sizing Rules

  • 2% Rule: Never risk >2% of credits on one trade
  • 5% Commodity: Max 5% of portfolio per commodity
  • 20% Sector: Max 20% in any market sector
  • 50% Trading: Keep 50% credits for opportunities

🚪 Exit Strategy Rules

  • Stop Loss: Sell if price drops 10-20%
  • Take Profit: Sell some at +30%, +50%, +100%
  • Time Limit: Review all positions weekly
  • Trend Change: Exit if trend reverses
