📊 Expected Value Calculations

🧮 Professional Risk Math

💰 Expected Value Formula

Expected Value Calculator
FORMULA EV = (Success Rate × Profit) - (Failure Rate × Loss)
EXAMPLE Gold Circuit: 70% success, 3,000cr profit
RISK 30% failure, 5,000cr loss (cargo + repairs)
EV CALC (0.7 × 3,000) - (0.3 × 5,000) = 600cr
DECISION Positive EV = Take the route!

🎯 Risk-Adjusted Profit Targets

  • Low Risk: 20-30% profit minimum
  • Medium Risk: 40-60% profit required
  • High Risk: 80-150% profit needed
  • Extreme Risk: 200%+ profit or don't bother
