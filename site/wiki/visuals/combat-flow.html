💡 Combat Flow Tips

🎯

Target Priority

Always attack the weakest enemy first! Reducing enemy numbers quickly means less damage coming your way.

🛡️

Shield Management

Your shields regenerate between battles. Sometimes it's better to flee and come back with full shields!

🚀

Know When to Run

There's no shame in escaping! If you're outgunned, use that fuel to jump to safety. Live to fight another day!
