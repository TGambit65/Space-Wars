🚀 Engine Components - Your Ship's Rocket Boosters!

What engines do: Engines are like the legs of your spaceship - they make you go fast, turn quickly, and basically let you zoom around the galaxy like the coolest race car driver ever! The better your engines, the faster you can escape from pirates or chase down awesome treasures! 🏎️💨

🌟 Engine Tier Rankings (From Awesome to SUPER AWESOME!)

⭐ Tier 1: Starter Engines
Beginner Friendly!

🔥 Chemical Thrusters

  • Speed: 2-3 sectors/hour (like a bicycle)
  • Power Need: 10-15 units (very low!)
  • Cost: 500-1,000 credits (super affordable!)
  • Best For: New pilots learning the ropes
Kid Tip: Perfect for your first ship! Like training wheels but for spaceships!
⭐⭐ Tier 2: Getting Better!
Good Choice!

⚡ Ion Drives

  • Speed: 4-6 sectors/hour (like a car)
  • Power Need: 20-25 units (reasonable)
  • Cost: 2,000-4,000 credits (worth saving for!)
  • Best For: Traders and explorers
Fun Fact: Ion drives use electricity to shoot tiny particles out the back - science is cool!
⭐⭐⭐ Tier 3: Now We're Talking!
Pro Level!

🌟 Fusion Engines

  • Speed: 8-12 sectors/hour (like a sports car!)
  • Power Need: 40-50 units (need good power system)
  • Cost: 8,000-15,000 credits (expensive but worth it!)
  • Best For: Combat ships and speed demons
Power Warning: These engines are hungry! Make sure your power system can feed them!
⭐⭐⭐⭐ Tier 4: LEGENDARY!
Epic Gear!

🌌 Quantum Drives

  • Speed: 15-25 sectors/hour (like a rocket!)
  • Power Need: 80-100 units (power monster!)
  • Cost: 25,000-50,000 credits (super expensive!)
  • Best For: Elite pilots with deep pockets
🏆 Legend Status: With these engines, you're faster than most pirates can blink!

⚡ Power Systems - Your Ship's Heart and Soul!

What power systems do: Think of power systems as your ship's stomach - they "eat" fuel and turn it into electricity to power everything else! Without power, your ship is basically a very expensive floating metal box. No lights, no engines, no weapons, no life support - just floating around like a sad space rock! 🔋💡

⚡ Power System Rankings (From Tiny Spark to MEGA POWER!)

⭐ Tier 1: Baby's First Power!
Super Cheap!

🔋 Battery Packs

  • Power Output: 15-25 units (like a big flashlight)
  • Duration: Limited! Runs out eventually!
  • Cost: 200-500 credits (super affordable!)
  • Best For: Emergency backup or tiny ships
⚠️ Battery Warning: These run out! Don't get stuck in space with dead batteries!
⭐⭐ Tier 2: Green Energy!
Eco-Friendly!

☀️ Solar Arrays

  • Power Output: 20-40 units (when sunny!)
  • Duration: Forever! (if there's sunlight)
  • Cost: 1,500-3,000 credits (reasonable)
  • Best For: Peaceful traders and explorers
🌟 Sunny Tip: These work great near stars but are useless in dark space!
⭐⭐⭐ Tier 3: Serious Power!
Reliable Choice!

🌟 Fusion Reactors

  • Power Output: 60-120 units (like a small power plant!)
  • Duration: Years and years with fuel
  • Cost: 10,000-20,000 credits (worth the investment!)
  • Best For: Combat ships and heavy-duty work
🏭 Pro Tip: This is what most serious pilots use - steady, reliable power!
⭐⭐⭐⭐ Tier 4: ULTIMATE POWER!
Epic Tier!

💥 Antimatter Cores

  • Power Output: 200-500 units (like a star!)
  • Duration: Decades with proper fuel
  • Cost: 40,000-100,000 credits (super expensive!)
  • Best For: Elite ships and dreadnoughts
💥 DANGER: If this explodes, your ship becomes a tiny sun! Handle with care!

💥 Weapon Systems - The PEW PEW Department!

What weapons do: Weapons are how you say "Hello!" to pirates and "Goodbye!" to asteroids! They're basically the coolest toys in space - laser guns, missile launchers, and other epic space weapons that would make any action movie hero jealous! Remember: weapons are for defending yourself and asteroid mining, not for being a space bully! 🚀💥

Installation Process

Installation Sequence: Components must be installed in the correct order for optimal performance.

Step-by-Step Installation

Installation Procedure
STEP 1 Install Power System (Required First)
STEP 2 Install Engine System (Required for mobility)
STEP 3 Install Core Systems (Life support, navigation)
STEP 4 Install Specialized Components (Weapons, cargo, etc.)
STEP 5 Verify Power Balance and System Integration

Compatibility Rules

  • Slot Matching: Components must match available slot types
  • Size Restrictions: Large components require multiple slots
  • Power Requirements: Total power draw cannot exceed generation
  • Hull Limitations: Some components are hull-type restricted
  • Tech Level: Advanced components require prerequisite technologies

Component Synergies

Certain component combinations provide bonus effects beyond their individual contributions:

Offensive Synergies

  • Targeting Computer + Weapons: +15% accuracy
  • Multiple Energy Weapons: Shared power efficiency
  • Fire Control System: Coordinated weapon timing

Defensive Synergies

  • Multiple Shields: Overlapping protection zones
  • Shield + Armor: Damage reduction combo
  • Point Defense + Shields: Missile protection

Common Installation Mistakes

Power Overload: Installing more components than your power system can support

Troubleshooting Guide

  • Component Won't Install: Check slot compatibility and prerequisites
  • Ship Won't Start: Verify power system is installed and functional
  • Poor Performance: Check for component conflicts or power shortages
  • System Failures: Review component dependencies and tech requirements
