⚡ Fighter Hull - The Space Motorcycle!

Combat Specialist

What it's like: This is your space motorcycle! Super fast, super agile, and perfect for zooming around space battling bad guys! It's like being a space ninja! 🥷

Cool Stats!

  • ❤️ Health: 100 (Not super tough, but tough enough!)
  • 🏃 Speed: 8 (ZOOM! Really fast!)
  • 🎯 Dodge Skills: 9 (Amazing at avoiding enemy shots!)
  • 📦 Storage: 20 (Only room for the essentials!)

Component Slots

  • Engine Slots: 2
  • Weapon Slots: 4
  • Shield Slots: 2
  • Utility Slots: 1
Best For: Hit-and-run tactics, escort missions, quick strikes

Role-Based Recommendations

Stat Priority Guide
  • Speed Priority: Fighter > Explorer > Freighter > Battleship
  • Durability Priority: Battleship > Freighter > Explorer > Fighter
  • Cargo Priority: Freighter > Explorer > Battleship > Fighter
  • Weapon Slots: Battleship > Fighter > Explorer > Freighter
  • Versatility: Explorer > Fighter > Freighter > Battleship

Advanced Hull Selection

Important: Hull choice affects upgrade paths and component compatibility.

Faction Considerations

Different factions may have access to unique hull variants or modifications. Consider your faction's strengths when selecting hulls.

Multi-Ship Strategies

Advanced players often maintain multiple ships for different purposes:

  • Main Ship: Your primary vessel for your preferred playstyle
  • Specialty Ships: Specialized vessels for specific missions
  • Fleet Ships: Additional vessels for fleet operations

Upgrade Considerations

Plan for future upgrades when selecting hulls. Some hulls offer better upgrade paths for certain roles than others.
