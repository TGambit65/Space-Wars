Ship Design Overview

Ship design in Space Wars 3000 follows a modular approach where you select a hull as your foundation, then install various components to customize performance for specific roles.

Design Process Workflow
  1. Select Hull: Choose a hull type that matches your intended role
  2. Install Core Components: Add engines, power systems, and life support
  3. Add Specialized Equipment: Install weapons, shields, or cargo systems
  4. Optimize Performance: Fine-tune component choices for maximum efficiency
  5. Test Configuration: Verify stats meet your requirements
Component Categories
  • Engines: Determine speed and maneuverability
  • Power Systems: Provide energy for all ship operations
  • Weapons: Offensive capabilities for combat
  • Shields: Defensive protection systems
  • Cargo: Storage capacity for trading
  • Utilities: Specialized equipment for various roles
Ship Design Quick Reference
HULLS Fighter | Freighter | Explorer | Battleship
SLOTS Engine | Power | Weapon | Shield | Cargo | Utility
ROLES Combat | Trading | Exploration | Multi-purpose
TIP Match hull type to intended role for optimal performance

Essential Design Tips

Power Management: Always ensure your power system can support all installed components.

For New Designers

  • Start with pre-built templates
  • Focus on one role at a time
  • Balance offense and defense
  • Don't forget about power requirements

Advanced Techniques

  • Min-max specific stats for specialization
  • Consider component synergies
  • Plan for upgrade paths
  • Test against different scenarios
