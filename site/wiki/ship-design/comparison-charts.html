🏃 Light Fighter

Beginner Friendly!

Best For: Speed & agility

  • ✅ Super fast
  • ✅ Very agile
  • ✅ Cheap to build
  • ❌ Low armor
  • ❌ Limited slots
Perfect for: New pilots, scouts, hit-and-run tactics

💪 Heavy Fighter

Balanced

Best For: All-around combat

  • ✅ More slots
  • ✅ Good armor
  • ✅ Versatile
  • ❌ Slower
  • ❌ More expensive
Perfect for: Main combat, balanced builds

⚡ Interceptor

Speed Demon!

Best For: Chasing enemies

  • ✅ Fastest ship
  • ✅ Ultra agile
  • ✅ Very cheap
  • ❌ Paper armor
  • ❌ Tiny (6 slots)
Perfect for: Pirate hunting, pursuit

💣 Bomber

Heavy Hitter!

Best For: Big damage

  • ✅ Heavy weapons
  • ✅ Good armor
  • ✅ Anti-capital
  • ❌ Very slow
  • ❌ Not agile
Perfect for: Station attacks, big targets

🛡️ Defense Systems Comparison

⚡ Shield Types

  • Basic Shield: Good all-around
  • Deflector: Anti-energy
  • Barrier: Anti-projectile
  • Adaptive: Learns & improves
Best on: Fast ships that regenerate between fights

🛡️ Armor Types

  • Standard: Cheap & reliable
  • Reactive: Explodes projectiles
  • Ablative: Burns off slowly
  • Nano-repair: Self-healing
Best on: Slow ships that take sustained damage

🌟 Special Defense

  • Cloak: Temporary invisibility
  • ECM: Missile confusion
  • Point Defense: Shoots projectiles
  • Phase Shift: Dodge attacks
Best on: Specialty builds with specific strategies

🎮 Playstyle Match Guide

🏴‍☠️ "I Want to Hunt Pirates!"

Recommended Ships

  1. 🥇 Heavy Fighter: Perfect balance of firepower and survival
  2. 🥈 Interceptor: Chase down fleeing pirates
  3. 🥉 Light Cruiser: Handle multiple pirates at once

Key Components

  • Fast-firing weapons (lasers)
  • Good shields for survivability
  • Scanner to find pirates
  • Tractor beam to catch loot
💰 "I Want to Get Rich Trading!"

Recommended Ships

  1. 🥇 Cargo Hauler: Maximum profit per trip
  2. 🥈 Fast Courier: Quick small profits
  3. 🥉 Armored Transport: Dangerous route specialist

Key Components

  • Cargo expansion modules
  • Efficient engines (save fuel)
  • Basic defenses
  • Market scanner
🔍 "I Want to Explore the Unknown!"

Recommended Ships

  1. 🥇 Explorer Cruiser: Built for deep space
  2. 🥈 Light Fighter: Fast scouting
  3. 🥉 Fast Courier: Quick exploration runs

Key Components

  • Advanced sensors
  • Long-range fuel tanks
  • Stealth systems
  • Emergency equipment
⚔️ "I Want Epic Space Battles!"

Recommended Ships

  1. 🥇 Battle Cruiser: Dominate large fights
  2. 🥈 Bomber: Destroy big targets
  3. 🥉 Heavy Fighter: Versatile combat

Key Components

  • Heavy weapons array
  • Layered defenses
  • Combat computer
  • Repair systems
