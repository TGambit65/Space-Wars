💥 Combat Efficiency Formulas

⚔️ Damage Per Second (DPS) Calculation

DPS Formula
FORMULA DPS = (Weapon Damage × Fire Rate) × Accuracy
EXAMPLE Laser: (100 damage × 2 shots/sec) × 0.8 accuracy = 160 DPS
MULTI-WEAPON Total DPS = Sum of all weapon DPS values

🛡️ Effective Hit Points (EHP) Calculation

EHP Formula
FORMULA EHP = Hull HP × (1 + Damage Reduction)
WITH SHIELDS EHP = (Hull HP + Shield HP) × (1 + DR)
EXAMPLE 1000 HP + 500 shields × 1.25 (25% armor) = 1,875 EHP

⏱️ Time To Kill (TTK) Optimization

TTK Formula
YOUR TTK TTK = Enemy EHP ÷ Your DPS
ENEMY TTK TTK = Your EHP ÷ Enemy DPS
WIN CONDITION You win if: Your TTK < Enemy TTK
💡 Pro Tip: The ship that kills faster wins! Focus on either increasing your DPS or your EHP to improve your combat efficiency! 🎯
💰 Economic Efficiency Formulas

📈 Trading Profit Optimization

Profit Formula
NET PROFIT Profit = (Sell Price - Buy Price) × Cargo - Fuel Cost
PROFIT/HOUR P/H = Net Profit ÷ (Travel Time + Trade Time)
EFFICIENCY Efficiency = Profit per Hour ÷ Ship Cost

⛽ Fuel Efficiency Optimization

Fuel Efficiency Formula
FUEL/SECTOR Fuel Use = Base Consumption × Mass Modifier × Engine Efficiency
OPTIMAL MASS Best Mass = √(Cargo Value ÷ Fuel Price)
BREAK-EVEN Min Profit = Fuel Cost × Safety Margin (1.5x)
🚀 Efficiency Secret: Better engines might cost more upfront but save thousands of credits in fuel over time! Do the math! 🧮
⚖️ Balance & Trade-off Formulas

🎯 Optimal Component Balance

Balance Ratios
POWER BALANCE Total Power Need ≤ Total Power Generation × 0.9
MASS BALANCE Speed = Base Speed × (1 - Mass/Max Mass)
HEAT BALANCE Heat Generation ≤ Heat Dissipation × 0.8

📊 Efficiency Score Calculation

The ultimate optimization formula that combines everything!

Master Efficiency Formula
COMBAT SCORE CS = (DPS × EHP) ÷ (Ship Cost × 100)
TRADE SCORE TS = (Cargo × Speed) ÷ (Fuel Use × 10)
OVERALL Ship Score = Role Weight × Primary Score + Secondary Scores

🎯 Min-Maxing Strategies (Become Overpowered!)

Min-maxing is the art of minimizing weaknesses while maximizing strengths - it's how you create ships that are absolutely unstoppable at their chosen role! Here are the secret strategies! 🏆

⚔️ Combat Min-Maxing

Advanced

Alpha Strike Build

  • MAX: First-shot damage (one-shot enemies)
  • MIN: Sustained combat ability
  • Formula: All power → biggest weapons
  • Result: Delete enemies before they shoot!

Tank Build

  • MAX: EHP and regeneration
  • MIN: Speed and damage
  • Formula: Stack armor + shields + repair
  • Result: Outlast any opponent!

💰 Economic Min-Maxing

Advanced

Speed Trader Build

  • MAX: Credits per hour
  • MIN: Everything else
  • Formula: Small cargo + max engines
  • Result: 10x trips = huge profits!

Bulk Hauler Build

  • MAX: Cargo capacity
  • MIN: Speed and defense
  • Formula: Every slot → cargo bays
  • Result: Move entire markets!

📐 Breakpoint Optimization

🎯 Finding Critical Breakpoints

Breakpoints are magic numbers where your ship suddenly becomes way more effective!

Common Breakpoints
2-SHOT BREAKPOINT Weapon damage = Enemy HP ÷ 2 (kill in 2 shots!)
SPEED BREAKPOINT Speed > 150 = Can outrun all pirates
TANK BREAKPOINT EHP > 5000 = Survive any alpha strike

💡 Breakpoint Strategy

  • Identify: Find the key numbers for your ship role
  • Calculate: How many components to reach breakpoint
  • Optimize: Use minimum components to hit breakpoint
  • Reinvest: Use saved slots for other improvements

📈 Case Study: Optimizing a Fighter for Maximum DPS

🎯 Starting Build (Unoptimized)

Basic Fighter Stats
WEAPONS 4x Basic Lasers = 200 DPS total
DEFENSE 2x Shields = 1000 EHP
EFFICIENCY DPS/Slot = 200/4 = 50 per slot

🔧 Optimization Process

  1. Replace basic lasers with plasma cannons: 2x Plasma = 300 DPS (2 slots)
  2. Add targeting computer: +30% accuracy = 390 DPS (3 slots)
  3. Add power amplifier: +25% damage = 487 DPS (4 slots)
  4. Result: 487 DPS in 4 slots = 121 DPS per slot!
💪 143% Improvement: Same number of slots, but 2.4x more damage! That's the power of optimization! 🚀
