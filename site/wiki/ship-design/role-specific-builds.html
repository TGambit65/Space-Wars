Customization Guidelines

Adaptation: These builds are starting points. Customize based on your playstyle and current game meta.

Customization Principles

  • Personal Playstyle: Adjust builds to match your preferred tactics
  • Current Meta: Adapt to common threats and strategies
  • Resource Availability: Use components you can actually obtain
  • Mission Requirements: Modify for specific operational needs
  • Fleet Synergy: Consider how your ship fits with others

Common Modifications

  • Budget Variants: Replace expensive components with cheaper alternatives
  • Tech Upgrades: Swap in better components as they become available
  • Situation Swaps: Modular components for different scenarios
  • Personal Preferences: Adjust for individual pilot skills and preferences
