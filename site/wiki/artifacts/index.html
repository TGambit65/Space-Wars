Artifacts

Artifacts are rare items found through exploration and combat that provide permanent bonuses when equipped to your ship.

Artifact Types

Rarity represents the relative drop chance. Lower percentages mean harder to find.

Finding Artifacts

Artifacts can be discovered through:

  • Combat loot — Defeating NPCs (especially rare types) may drop artifacts
  • Planet scanning — Detailed scans of planets can reveal hidden artifacts
  • Exploration — Visiting uncharted systems increases discovery chances

Equipping Artifacts

Artifacts are equipped through the Ships panel in the Artifacts section. Each artifact provides a passive bonus that stacks with your existing ship stats.

Tips

Fusion Catalyst (12% rarity) is the most common artifact and one of the most useful — +20% fuel efficiency saves credits on every jump.

Temporal Fragment (2% rarity) is the rarest artifact. Its -10% research time bonus stacks well with Research Lab bonuses from colonies.

Void Crystal (+12% weapon damage) and Quantum Stabilizer (+15% shield capacity) are the best combat artifacts. Prioritize these for warships.
