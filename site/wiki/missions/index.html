Missions

Missions are tasks offered by ports that reward credits and XP upon completion. They provide structured goals and bonus income beyond regular trading.

Mission Types

TypeCredit MultiplierXP MultiplierDescription
Delivery1.0×1.0×Transport cargo to a destination port
Bounty1.5×1.5×Destroy a specific NPC target
Scan0.8×1.2×Scan planets or sectors for data
Trade Volume1.2×0.8×Buy/sell a required volume of goods
Patrol1.0×1.0×Visit and secure specific sectors

Rules

  • Max active missions: 5 at a time
  • Missions per port: 3 available at each port
  • Expiration: 24 hours from acceptance
  • Mission refresh: Ports generate new missions every 30 minutes

Tips

Bounty missions offer the best combined rewards (1.5× credits and XP) but require combat capability.

Scan missions are great for explorers — high XP with lower risk.

Stack missions that share destinations. A delivery mission and a trade volume mission to the same region can be completed in one trip.

Check ports frequently — missions refresh every 30 minutes, so new opportunities appear regularly.
