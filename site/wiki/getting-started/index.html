Getting Started

Getting Started

Welcome to Space Wars 3000, an MMO space trading and combat game. This guide will walk you through your first steps in the galaxy.

Creating Your Account

  1. Navigate to the game login page
  2. Enter a username and password to register
  3. You'll be logged in automatically and taken to the Dashboard

The Dashboard

Your dashboard is your command center. It shows:

  • Your current sector — where you are in the galaxy
  • Ship status — hull, shields, fuel, and cargo
  • Credits — your available funds
  • Quick actions — jump to common tasks

Your Starting Ship

Every new pilot begins with a Scout — a versatile light ship:

StatValue
Hull100
Shields50
Fuel100
Cargo50
Crew Capacity2

The Scout has 1 weapon slot, 1 shield slot, 1 engine slot, 2 scanner slots, 1 cargo pod slot, and 1 armor slot. It's ideal for exploration and light trading.

Your First Steps

1. Check Your System

Click System in the sidebar to view the 3D system view of your current star system. You'll see:

  • The central star
  • Orbiting planets (unscanned ones appear as dim anonymous spheres)
  • Any ports or NPCs in the area

2. Explore the Galaxy Map

Click Sector Map to open the galaxy map. The galaxy contains ~400 star systems connected by hyperlanes. You can:

  • Click a connected system to jump there (costs fuel)
  • Click your current system to enter the system view
  • Look for wormholes that connect distant systems

3. Start Trading

Visit a port in your system to begin trading. Buy low at one port type, sell high at another. Check the Trading guide for details.

4. Upgrade Your Ship

Earn credits through trading and visit the Shipyard to install better components. See Ship Designer for component details.

Key Tips

Fuel Management: Always keep enough fuel to reach a Fuel Depot. Running out of fuel in deep space is dangerous.

Save Credits: Your starting credits are limited. Focus on profitable trade routes before investing in combat upgrades.

Scan Everything: Use your scanners to reveal planets, which may be colonizable or contain valuable resources.
