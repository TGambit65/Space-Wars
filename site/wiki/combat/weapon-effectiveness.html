🎯 Damage Type Details

⚡ Energy Weapons (Lasers)

Shield Specialist

Strengths:

  • 150% damage to shields
  • Instant hit (no travel time)
  • Perfect accuracy
  • No ammunition needed

Weaknesses:

  • 50% damage to armor
  • Power hungry
  • Heat buildup issues
Use When: Enemy has strong shields that need breaking!

🚀 Projectile Weapons (Missiles)

Hull Destroyer

Strengths:

  • 150% damage to hull
  • Area damage potential
  • Guided tracking
  • High burst damage

Weaknesses:

  • Can be shot down
  • Limited ammunition
  • Travel time to target
Use When: Shields are down and you want quick kills!

🎯 Range Categories Explained

🔍 Close Range Weapons (0-300 units)

Perfect for Dogfights!

Best Close-Range Weapons
  • Pulse Lasers: Rapid fire devastation
  • Scatter Guns: Can't miss at this range
  • Flame Throwers: Continuous damage
  • Ion Blasters: System disruption
Close Range Tactics
  • Get in fast, hit hard
  • Circle-strafe enemies
  • Use superior agility
  • Overwhelm with DPS
⚖️ Medium Range Weapons (300-700 units)

The Versatile Choice!

Best Medium-Range Weapons
  • Plasma Cannons: Balanced performance
  • Standard Missiles: Reliable damage
  • Beam Lasers: Sustained fire
  • Autocannons: Good vs everything
Medium Range Tactics
  • Maintain optimal distance
  • Kite slower enemies
  • Control engagement
  • Flexible positioning
🎯 Long Range Weapons (700+ units)

Sniper's Paradise!

Best Long-Range Weapons
  • Railguns: Extreme range alpha
  • Artillery Missiles: Indirect fire
  • Siege Lasers: Station killers
  • Torpedo Launchers: Capital ship hunters
Long Range Tactics

🎮 Situational Weapon Selection Guide

Choosing the right weapon for the situation is half the battle! Here's when to use each weapon type for maximum effectiveness! 🎯

🎭 Combat Scenario Recommendations

🏴‍☠️ Fighting Pirates (Small, Fast Targets)

Recommended Loadout

  • Primary: Pulse Lasers - High accuracy, instant hit
  • Secondary: Guided Missiles - For runners
  • Support: Point Defense - Stop their missiles
Anti-Pirate Strategy
TACTIC Use high-accuracy weapons to counter their speed
RANGE Stay at medium range - they're weak up close
TARGET Focus engines first to prevent escape
🚢 Capital Ship Combat (Large, Slow Targets)

Recommended Loadout

  • Primary: Torpedoes - Maximum hull damage
  • Secondary: Railguns - Long range punishment
  • Support: Bombers - Disable subsystems
Anti-Capital Strategy
TACTIC Use slow, high-damage weapons - they can't dodge
RANGE Maximum range to avoid their heavy weapons
TARGET Weapons first, then engines, then bridge
🛡️ Breaking Turtle Ships (Heavy Defense)

Recommended Loadout

  • Primary: Mixed damage types - Find their weakness
  • Secondary: Sustained DPS weapons - Wear them down
  • Support: Shield disruptors - Remove defenses
Anti-Tank Strategy
PHASE 1 Energy weapons to drop shields
PHASE 2 Kinetic weapons to crack armor
PHASE 3 Explosive weapons to finish hull

🎯 Golden Rules of Weapon Selection

  • 📊 Always mix damage types for versatility
  • 🎯 Match weapon range to your combat style
  • ⚡ High DPS for close combat, high alpha for ambush
  • 🔄 Have a plan for shields AND armor
  • 💰 Consider ammo costs for projectile weapons
  • 🔥 Synergize weapons with your ship's strengths
