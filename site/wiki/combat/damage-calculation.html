📐 The Basic Damage Formula

🎯 The Golden Rule: Final Damage = Base Damage × All Modifiers - All Reductions! Understanding this formula is the key to combat mastery! 🔑

Damage calculation in Space Wars 3000 follows a specific order of operations. Each step can dramatically change the final number that pops up when you hit an enemy! Let's break it down!

Master Damage Formula
STEP 1 Base Damage = Weapon's listed damage value
STEP 2 Modified Damage = Base × (1 + Damage Bonuses)
STEP 3 Critical Check = Roll for critical hit chance
STEP 4 Resistance Check = Apply enemy resistances
STEP 5 Final Damage = Result after all calculations

🎲 Step-by-Step Example

📊 Example: Laser Cannon Shot

The Setup

You fire a Plasma Cannon at an enemy fighter. Let's calculate the damage!

Damage Calculation Example
BASE DAMAGE Plasma Cannon = 100 damage
YOUR BONUSES +20% from skills, +15% from targeting computer
CALCULATION 100 × (1 + 0.20 + 0.15) = 100 × 1.35 = 135
CRIT ROLL 20% chance... SUCCESS! Damage × 2 = 270
ENEMY ARMOR 25% plasma resistance: 270 × 0.75 = 202.5
FINAL DAMAGE 203 damage dealt! (rounded up)
💡 What Happened: Your 100 base damage became 203 through bonuses and a critical hit, even after enemy resistance! That's a 103% increase! 🎯

🎯 Damage Modifiers Explained

Damage modifiers are what separate good pilots from GREAT ones! These multipliers can turn weak attacks into devastating strikes! Let's learn about each type! 🚀

⬆️ Positive Modifiers

Increase Damage

Common Damage Bonuses

  • Skill Bonuses: +5% to +50%
  • Equipment Mods: +10% to +30%
  • Range Bonus: +25% at optimal range
  • Faction Bonus: +15% with faction weapons
  • Formation Bonus: +20% in attack formation
🔥 Stack 'Em Up: Most bonuses stack additively! +20% + 15% = +35% total!

⬇️ Negative Modifiers

Reduce Damage

Common Damage Penalties

  • Long Range: -50% beyond optimal
  • Moving Target: -20% vs fast ships
  • Damaged Weapons: -30% if damaged
  • Power Shortage: -40% if low power
  • Weather Effects: -15% in nebulas
😰 Watch Out: Penalties can stack too! Keep your ship in good condition!

🎰 Range Modifiers

Weapon Range Effectiveness

🎲 How Critical Hits Work

The Critical Hit System

Critical Hit Mechanics
BASE CHANCE All weapons start with 5% crit chance
SKILL BONUS Targeting skills add +1-10% crit chance
EQUIPMENT Targeting computer: +5% crit chance
WEAPON MODS Precision barrel: +3% crit chance
MAX CHANCE Crit chance caps at 50% (still random!)

Critical Damage Multipliers

  • Normal Critical: 2.0x damage (double damage!)
  • Precision Critical: 2.5x damage (with special skills)
  • Devastating Critical: 3.0x damage (legendary weapons only)
  • System Critical: 2.0x damage + disables a system!
💥 Pro Tip: Stack crit chance bonuses to turn every third shot into a critical hit! That's a 66% damage increase overall! 🎯

Where You Hit Matters!

🛡️ Resistance Calculations

📊 How Resistances Work

Resistance Formula

Resistance Calculation
FORMULA Final Damage = Incoming × (1 - Resistance%)
EXAMPLE 100 damage vs 30% resist = 100 × 0.7 = 70
STACKING Multiple resists multiply: 20% + 20% = 36% total
CAP Maximum resistance is 75% (always take some damage)

Common Resistance Sources

  • Armor Type: 10-30% to specific damage
  • Shield Mods: 5-15% resistance bonus
  • Hull Material: 10-20% inherent resistance
  • Active Systems: 15-25% when powered

📈 Maximize Your Damage

Offensive Tips
  1. Stack Bonuses: Combine all damage modifiers

Defensive Tips

  4. Use Cover: Asteroids block shots!
  5. Emergency Systems: Save defensive cooldowns
🛡️ Example: 30% resist + moving + cover = take only 40% damage!
