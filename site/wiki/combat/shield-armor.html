🛡️ Shield & Armor Mechanics: Your Space Survival Guide!

🏰 Defensive Systems Academy: Welcome to the complete guide on not exploding! Learn how shields and armor work together to keep you alive in space battles! 💫🚀

In space, the difference between victory and becoming space dust often comes down to your defenses! Shields and armor aren't just health bars - they're complex systems with strengths, weaknesses, and cool interactions. Think of shields as your energy bubble and armor as your metal skin. Together, they create layers of protection that can turn you into an unstoppable space fortress! Let's learn how to never explode again! 🛡️⚡

⚡ How Shields Work (Your Energy Bubble!)

🌟 Shield Basics: Shields are energy fields that absorb damage before it hits your ship. They regenerate over time but can be overloaded! Think of them as rechargeable armor! 🔋

Shields are your first line of defense - they take damage so your ship doesn't have to! But shields aren't just simple hit points. They have special properties that make them amazing against some attacks and terrible against others! Let's dive into the science! 🔬

Shield System Properties
PROPERTY DESCRIPTION | EFFECT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
CAPACITY Total shield HP | How much damage before failing
REGEN RATE HP per second | How fast shields come back
REGEN DELAY Seconds after hit | Wait time before regen starts
RESISTANCE Damage reduction | % less damage from certain types

🌟 Shield Overload Effects

When shields take too much damage too fast, they can overload!

  • Overload Threshold: 50% capacity in 1 second
  • Overload Effect: Shields drop to 0 instantly
  • Recovery Time: 2x normal regen delay
  • Prevention: Layer shields or use capacitors

🛡️ How Armor Works (Your Metal Skin!)

🔩 Armor Basics: Unlike shields, armor doesn't regenerate - but it provides consistent protection and can't be overloaded! Think of it as permanent damage reduction! 🏗️

Armor is your ship's tough outer shell. While it doesn't regenerate like shields, armor provides reliable protection that works differently - it reduces incoming damage rather than absorbing it! 🛡️

🎯 Armor Penetration Mechanics

How Armor Penetration Works

Some weapons are designed to punch through armor like it's paper! Here's how it works:

Penetration Calculation
STEP 1 Check weapon's AP (Armor Penetration) value
STEP 2 Compare to target's armor thickness
IF AP > ARMOR Full damage bypasses armor!
IF AP < ARMOR Damage reduced by armor %

🔫 Weapons with Armor Penetration

  • Railgun: 80% AP - Goes through most armor
  • Gauss Cannon: 60% AP - Good vs medium armor
  • AP Missiles: 50% AP - Specialized rounds
  • Plasma: 30% AP - Some penetration

🔄 Shield Cycling Strategy

How Shield Cycling Works

Use multiple shield generators that activate in sequence!

  • Primary Shield: Takes damage first
  • Secondary Shield: Activates when primary fails
  • Primary Regenerates: While secondary is active
  • Swap Back: Infinite shield regeneration!
🔄 Pro Tip: With 3 shield generators, you can have nearly permanent shield coverage in extended fights!

Slow but steady healing!

  • Works on armor only
  • Always active

Best Use: Long battles and sieges
