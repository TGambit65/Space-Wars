💡 Achievement Hunting Tips

🎯 General Tips

  • Check achievement requirements before starting
  • Some achievements stack - plan accordingly
  • Save before attempting difficult achievements
  • Join achievement hunting groups for help

🚀 Efficiency Guide

  • Complete faction achievements in one playthrough
  • Combat achievements pair well with pirate hunting
  • Trading achievements fund everything else
  • Use multiple saves for conflicting achievements
