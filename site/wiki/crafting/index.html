Crafting

Crafting lets you create components and commodities from raw materials. It requires the Basic Crafting technology.

Requirements

  1. Research the Basic Crafting technology (5,000 credits, 30 minutes)
  2. Have the required ingredients in your cargo
  3. Meet the minimum character level for the blueprint
  4. Have enough credits for the crafting fee

Tips

Refine Metals is available at level 1 with Basic Crafting — it's a great early money-maker. Buy cheap Ore at Mining Outposts and refine it into Refined Metals worth 80 credits each.

Component crafting at level 3 saves significant credits compared to buying from ports. A Pulse Laser costs 1,200 to buy but only ~500 in materials + fees to craft.

AI Core fabrication requires Nanomaterials (650 base price), but AI Cores sell for 800+ base. High-level crafters can profit from this conversion.
