🚀 Ship Design Workflow

Optimal Ship Design Process:

  1. Define Purpose: What will this ship do?
  2. Use Ship Calculator: Build and optimize your design
  3. Test in Combat Simulator: Verify performance
  4. Plan Trade Routes: If it's a trader, map profitable routes
  5. Refine Design: Adjust based on test results
Design Workflow Timeline
STEP 1 Ship Calculator: 15-20 minutes
STEP 2 Combat Simulator: 10-15 minutes
STEP 3 Trade Planner: 5-10 minutes
TOTAL 30-45 minutes for perfect ship
💰 Trading Strategy Workflow

Profitable Trading Process:

  1. Design Trading Ship: Use Ship Calculator for cargo optimization
  2. Plan Routes: Use Trade Planner for maximum profit
  3. Test Defense: Use Combat Simulator for pirate encounters
  4. Calculate Profits: Use Damage Calculator for insurance costs
  5. Execute Plan: Apply your strategy in-game
💡 Trading Secret: The best traders use ALL the tools together! A well-designed ship on a well-planned route with good defenses makes the most money!
