💰 Economic Foundation

Building Wealth

  • Trade Routes: Establish profitable commodity flows early
  • Resource Control: Secure high-value resource nodes
  • Infrastructure: Invest in economic multipliers
  • Efficiency: Minimize waste and maximize returns
Economic Rule: A 10% economic advantage compounds into overwhelming superiority over time.
🗺️ Expansion Strategy

Territory Management

  • Strategic Locations: Control chokepoints and resource hubs
  • Defensive Depth: Create buffer zones around core systems
  • Forward Bases: Project power into new regions
  • Supply Lines: Maintain secure logistics routes
PRIORITY Quality over quantity in early expansion
TIMING Expand during opponent weakness

Common Strategic Approaches

🐢 Turtle Strategy

Overview

Focus on defense and economic development before expanding.

  • Advantages: Strong economy, difficult to defeat
  • Disadvantages: Slow expansion, limited early influence
  • Best For: Economic or Technology victories
  • Counters: Early aggression, territory denial
🦅 Rush Strategy

Overview

Early military aggression to cripple opponents before they develop.

  • Advantages: Quick victories, resource capture
  • Disadvantages: Economic weakness if failed
  • Best For: Military victory, aggressive factions
  • Counters: Strong early defense, alliances
🌊 Boom Strategy

Overview

Rapid expansion to claim as much territory as possible.

  • Advantages: Resource control, strategic positioning
  • Disadvantages: Overextension risk, weak defenses
  • Best For: Large map games, exploration bonuses
  • Counters: Targeted strikes, economic warfare

Combat Tips

  • Fleet Composition: Mix ship types for balanced capabilities
  • Repair Priority: Damaged ships are liabilities in combat
  • Ambush Points: Use asteroid fields and nebulae
  • Retreat Option: Live to fight another day

Economic Tips

  • Trade Timing: Buy low during surplus, sell high during shortage
  • Infrastructure First: Economic buildings multiply all gains
  • Automation: Set up recurring trade routes
  • Market Watch: Monitor price trends across sectors

Diplomatic Tips

  • First Impressions: Initial actions set faction attitudes
  • Gift Economy: Small gifts build long-term relations
  • Common Enemies: Nothing unites like shared threats
  • Trust Building: Honor agreements to build reputation
