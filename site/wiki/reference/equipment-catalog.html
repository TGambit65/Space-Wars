✨ Special Equipment

🎪 Fun Equipment: These aren't just upgrades - they're game-changers! Some of these gadgets are so cool, they're practically cheating (but totally legal)!

🌟 Cloaking Device

  • Effect: Become invisible for 30 seconds
  • Cooldown: 5 minutes
  • Energy Cost: 50% of max
  • Best For: Sneaky escapes and ambushes
  • Fun Fact: Pirates hate this one weird trick!

⚡ Emergency Teleporter

  • Effect: Instantly jump 1 sector away
  • Uses: Single use per mission
  • Activation: When hull < 20%
  • Best For: Not dying!
  • Warning: Random destination!

🔧 Repair Drones

  • Effect: Auto-repair 5 HP/second
  • Duration: Continuous
  • Capacity: 3 drones max
  • Best For: Long missions
  • Bonus: They're cute little robots!

📡 Advanced Scanner

  • Effect: Reveals hidden objects
  • Range: Entire sector
  • Bonus: +50% loot discovery
  • Best For: Treasure hunters
  • Secret: Can find secret jump points!
