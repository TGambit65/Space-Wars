Colonization Guide

How to find the perfect planet, establish a colony, and build it into a thriving settlement.

Step 1: Finding a Planet

Best Planet Types for Colonies

Planet TypeHabitabilityWhy Choose It
Terran100%Fastest development, best population growth
Jungle80%High habitability + Organics, Bio-Samples, Food, Seeds
Oceanic70%Good habitability + Water, Food, Bio-Samples
Desert50%Moderate habitability + Rare Minerals, Ore

Where to Look

  • Class G (Yellow) stars — Up to 15 planets, highest Terran/Oceanic/Jungle chance
  • Class F (Yellow-White) stars — Up to 10 planets, good habitable mix
  • Mid-orbit positions (4–9) — Best chance for habitable planet types

Equip your ship with good scanners (Deep Scanner or better) and systematically explore G-class and F-class systems.

Recommendation: Use a regular Colony Ship if you can wait. The larger cargo and crew capacity give your colony a better start.

Step 3: Founding the Colony

  1. Load your Colony Ship with supplies (Food, Water, Construction Materials)
  2. Navigate to the target system
  3. Approach the planet
  4. Pay the 10,000 credit colonization fee
  5. Your Colony Ship is consumed — make sure you have another ship available!

Development Timer

For standard Colony Ships, the colony enters a development phase:

  • Duration scales with planet habitability (lower habitability = longer timer)
  • Maximum development time is 8 hours (for the harshest worlds)
  • Terran planets develop fastest

Step 4: Building Strategy

Phase 1: Power & Water (Infrastructure 1)

Build first:

  1. Solar Array (15,000) — Generates 100 power, no power consumption
  2. Water Pump (20,000) — Produces 25 Water, no power needed

Phase 2: Food & Population (Infrastructure 2)

  1. Hydroponic Farm (20,000) — Converts 5 Water into 30 Food
  2. Habitat Module (30,000) — +500 max population
  3. Entertainment Complex (25,000) — +10% morale

Phase 3: Industry (Infrastructure 3)

  1. Surface Mine (25,000) — Produces 20 Ore
  2. Geothermal Plant (40,000) — 250 power generation
  3. Refinery (60,000) — Converts 10 Ore into 8 Refined Metals
  4. Spaceport (45,000) — +25% trade capacity

Phase 4: Advanced (Infrastructure 5+)

  1. Deep Core Drill (upgrade from Surface Mine) — 50 Ore output
  2. Research Lab (50,000) — +15% research speed
  3. Component Factory (100,000) — Converts metals + electronics into Ship Parts

Phase 5: Endgame (Infrastructure 6+)

  1. Fusion Reactor (100,000) — 500 power generation
  2. Quantum Extractor (upgrade from Deep Core Drill) — 120 Ore output
  3. Begin Wonder construction (requires Advanced Colonies tech)
