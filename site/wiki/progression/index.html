Crew & Progression

Progression

Space Wars 3000 features a leveling system, skill trees, and a technology tree that unlock new abilities and improvements as you play.

Leveling

Your character earns XP from combat, trading, and missions. Each level grants 1 skill point.

Skills

Spend skill points on 8 skills. Each skill has a maximum level of 10.

SkillEffect per LevelDescription
Combat Mastery+3% weapon damageIncreases all weapon damage output
Shield Expertise+4% shield capacityBoosts total shield hit points
Trade Acumen+2% trade profitBetter buy/sell prices at ports
Navigation-3% fuel usageReduces fuel consumed per jump
Engineering+5% repair effectivenessCheaper and better repairs
Leadership+2% crew effectivenessAll crew bonuses improved
Mining Proficiency+5% resource extractionMore resources from colonies
Scanner Mastery+4% scan rangeExtended scanning distance

Tip: Early on, invest in Navigation and Trade Acumen to maximize profits. Shift to Combat Mastery and Shield Expertise once you're ready for tougher fights.
