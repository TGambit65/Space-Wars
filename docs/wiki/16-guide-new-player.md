# New Player Guide

Your first 30 minutes in Space Wars 3000 — a step-by-step walkthrough.

## Minutes 0–5: Orientation

1. **Register and log in** — You start with a Scout ship, some credits, and 10 Food + 5 Water in cargo
2. **Check the Dashboard** — Note your starting sector, credits, and ship stats
3. **Open the System view** — Click "System" in the sidebar to see your current star system in 3D

## Minutes 5–10: First Scan

1. **Scan planets** — Use your scanner to reveal nearby planets. Unscanned planets show as "SIG-DETECTED"
2. **Check for ports** — Look for the octahedron shapes in the system view — these are trading ports
3. **Note the port type** — This determines what you can buy and sell here

## Minutes 10–20: First Trade Run

1. **Sell your starting cargo** — Sell the 10 Food and 5 Water at the nearest port for quick credits
2. **Check port prices** — Go to the Trading page to see what's cheap here and expensive elsewhere
3. **Buy low** — Purchase commodities the port produces (low price)
4. **Open the Sector Map** — Look for a nearby system with a different port type
5. **Jump there** — Click the connected system (watch your fuel!)
6. **Sell high** — Sell your cargo at the destination port

### Profitable Early Routes

| Buy At | Commodity | Sell At | Profit Margin |
|--------|-----------|---------|---------------|
| Mining Outpost | Ore (25 base) | Tech Center | High |
| Agricultural Station | Food (30 base) | Mining Outpost | Moderate |
| Fuel Depot | Fuel (50 base) | Any port | Moderate |
| Tech Center | Electronics (200 base) | Agricultural Station | High |

## Minutes 20–25: Ship Upgrades

With your first profits:

1. **Visit the Shipyard** — Install better components
2. **Priority upgrades**:
   - **Engine** — Plasma Drive (800 credits) for better fuel efficiency
   - **Scanner** — Long-Range Scanner (600 credits) to reveal more planets
   - **Cargo Pod** — Small Cargo Pod (250 credits) to carry more goods

## Minutes 25–30: Plan Your Path

Decide your direction:

- **Trader path**: Save for a Merchant Cruiser (more cargo pods) and keep running trade routes
- **Explorer path**: Upgrade scanners, explore the galaxy map for valuable systems
- **Combat path**: Install weapons, pick fights with Pirates for loot and XP
- **Colony path**: Save the 10,000+ credits needed to buy a Colony Ship and find a habitable planet

## Key Reminders

> **Always keep fuel reserves** — Getting stranded is the #1 new player mistake. Never jump if it will leave you with less than 20% fuel.

> **Repair regularly** — Components degrade with every jump (0.5%) and combat round (2%). Visit Engineering before things break.

> **Check the wiki** — This wiki has detailed information on every game system. Use the sidebar to explore topics.
