# Crew

Crew members provide bonuses to your ship's performance. Each crew member has a species, a role, and stat bonuses that affect gameplay.

## Roles

Each crew member is assigned one of 4 roles:

| Role | Primary Stat | Effect |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Pilot | Piloting | Improves ship speed and flee chance |
| Engineer | Engineering | Reduces maintenance costs and improves repairs |
| Gunner | Combat | Increases weapon accuracy and damage |
| Scientist | Science | Improves scanning and artifact research |

## Hiring

- **Hiring fee** = Base salary × 5 (one-time cost)
- Crew are hired at ports
- Your ship's crew capacity limits how many crew you can have

## Salary

- Crew are paid their base salary once every **24 hours**
- The salary multiplier is server-configurable (default 1.0×)
- Make sure you can afford your crew's wages before hiring

## Optimal Crew Loadouts

**Combat Ship**: Zorath or Combat Droid gunners for maximum damage. Vexian pilot for flee chance.

**Trade Ship**: Grox or Worker Bot engineers to reduce maintenance. Human crew for balanced performance.

**Explorer**: Sylphi or Science Unit scientists for scan bonuses. Void Walker pilot for speed.
