# Trading

## Port Types

There are 8 types of ports, each with different specializations:

| Port Type | Tax Rate | Behavior |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Trading Hub | 5% | Buys and sells almost everything |
| Mining Outpost | 3% | Produces: Ore, Refined Metals, Rare Minerals. Consumes: Food, Water, Equipment, Construction Materials |
| Agricultural Station | 4% | Produces: Food, Organics, Seeds, Livestock, Water. Consumes: Chemicals, Construction Materials, Electronics |
| Tech Center | 8% | Produces: Electronics, Ship Parts, AI Cores, Nav Data. Consumes: Refined Metals, Chemicals, Plasma, Rare Minerals |
| Space Station | 6% | General purpose — buys and sells with 50% commodity availability |
| Fuel Depot | 2% | Produces: Fuel, Oxygen, Plasma. Consumes: Ore, Chemicals, Water |
| Medical Station | 7% | Produces: Medical Supplies, Bio-Samples. Consumes: Chemicals, Electronics, Organics, Water |
| Black Market | 10% | Trades everything including contraband. 40% commodity availability |

## Pricing

Prices are dynamic and fluctuate based on supply and demand:

- **Base price** — Each commodity has a base price (shown in the tables above)
- **Price range** — Actual prices vary from **0.5×** to **2.0×** the base price
- **Volatility** — Higher volatility means bigger price swings
- **Price spread** — The difference between buy and sell prices is 15%
- **Tax** — Each port charges a tax rate on transactions (2%–10%)

### Profitable Trading

The most profitable trades come from buying at a port that **produces** a commodity (low price) and selling at a port that **consumes** it (high price). For example:

- Buy **Ore** at a Mining Outpost → Sell at a Tech Center
- Buy **Electronics** at a Tech Center → Sell at an Agricultural Station
- Buy **Food** at an Agricultural Station → Sell at a Mining Outpost
