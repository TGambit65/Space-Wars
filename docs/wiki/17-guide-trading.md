# Trading Guide

Advanced strategies for maximizing profit in Space Wars 3000.

## Understanding the Market

### Price Dynamics

Prices fluctuate between **0.5×** and **2.0×** base price. Key factors:
- **Volatility** — High-volatility goods (Alien Artifacts at 0.6) swing more than stable ones (Oxygen at 0.15)
- **Supply/demand** — Ports that produce a commodity sell it cheap; ports that consume it buy it dear
- **Price spread** — There's always a 15% spread between buy and sell prices

## Best Trade Routes

### The Industrial Triangle

The most reliable profit loop:

1. **Mining Outpost** → Buy Ore (25 base)
2. **Tech Center** → Sell Ore, buy Electronics (200 base)
3. **Agricultural Station** → Sell Electronics, buy Food (30 base)
4. **Mining Outpost** → Sell Food, buy Ore again

Each leg generates profit. With a Freighter (500 cargo), this loop can earn thousands per cycle.

### High-Value Runs

For players with large cargo capacity:

| Route | Buy | Sell | Base Profit per Unit |
|-------|-----|------|---------------------|
| Tech → Any | AI Cores (800) | Consumer ports | High volatility = big swings |
| Any → Medical | Chemicals (60) | Medical Station | Reliable demand |
| Mining → Tech | Rare Minerals (600) | Tech Center consumes | Premium goods |
| Agricultural → Mining | Food + Water | Mining Outpost | Low risk, steady |

### Contraband

Black Markets trade contraband at high margins but with 10% tax:

| Item | Base Price | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Narcotics | 450 | High volatility, big profits |
| Clone Organs | 700 | Highest value contraband |
| Stolen Goods | 200 | Low price, easy to stack |
| Counterfeit Credits | 300 | Moderate risk/reward |

> **Warning**: Contraband can only be traded at Black Markets.

## Efficiency Tips

> **Use the Market Data page** to compare prices across known ports before committing to a route.

> **Invest in engines** — Better fuel efficiency means more profit per trip by reducing fuel costs.

> **Trade Acumen skill** (+2% per level) stacks up to +20% at max level. Prioritize this for dedicated traders.

> **Automate profitable routes** once you unlock Basic Automation tech, but remember the 1.5× fuel multiplier.
