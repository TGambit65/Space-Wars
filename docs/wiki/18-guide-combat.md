# Combat Guide

How to build an effective combat ship and survive encounters in Space Wars 3000.

## Know Your Enemy

### NPC Threat Tiers

| Tier | NPC | Ship | Danger Level |
|------|-----|------|-------------|
| Easy | Trader | Freighter, Merchant Cruiser | Low — they flee |
| Medium | Pirate | Fighter, Corvette | Moderate — aggressive |
| Medium | System Patrol | Corvette, Fighter | Only if provoked |
| Hard | Bounty Hunter | Fighter, Corvette | Opportunistic — well-equipped |
| Deadly | Pirate Lord | Destroyer | Boss-tier — avoid until ready |

### When to Fight

- **Traders**: Safe to engage. They try to flee and have low loot (0.8× multiplier)
- **Pirates**: Good targets once you have a combat-fitted Corvette. 1.5× loot
- **Bounty Hunters**: Only engage if you're confident. 2.0× loot
- **Pirate Lords**: Need a Destroyer or Battlecruiser to fight safely. 3.0× loot

## Combat Tactics

### Damage Optimization

- **High damage, lower accuracy** weapons (Graviton Lance: 120 dmg, 50% acc) are better against heavily armored targets
- **Ion Cannons** deal 1.5× damage to shields — great for stripping defenses
- **Critical hits** (10% chance, 2.0× damage) can swing fights — more weapons = more crit chances

### Defense Strategy

- **Regenerative Shields** (12/round recharge) are best for sustained fights
- **Phase Shields** (500 capacity) are best for burst damage absorption
- **Heavy Shields** (250 capacity, 3 recharge) — compromise between capacity and cost
- 10% of all damage bypasses shields, so armor matters even with full shields

### When to Flee

- **Base flee chance**: 30%
- **Speed matters**: +5% per speed point advantage
- **Interceptors** with 3 engine slots and Hyperspace Drives can almost always escape
- **Flee early** if you're losing shields fast — don't wait until hull damage

## Skills for Combat

Priority skill investments:
1. **Combat Mastery** (+3%/level) — Direct damage increase
2. **Shield Expertise** (+4%/level) — More shield HP
3. **Engineering** (+5%/level) — Cheaper post-combat repairs
