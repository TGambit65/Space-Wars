# Corporations

Corporations are player organizations that enable group play, shared resources, and coordinated activities.

## Creating a Corporation

- **Cost**: 50,000 credits
- **Name**: 3–50 characters
- **Tag**: 2–10 characters (displayed next to member names)

The founder becomes the corporation's CEO.

## Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|------------|
| CEO | Full control — manage members, treasury, settings |
| Officer | Invite/kick members, manage treasury |
| Member | Access shared resources, participate in corp activities |

## Limits

- **Maximum members**: 20 per corporation
- Members can only belong to one corporation at a time

## Treasury

The corporation has a shared treasury that officers and the CEO can manage. Members can contribute credits to fund group operations.

## Tips

> **Pool resources** with your corp to fund expensive purchases like Colony Ships or Battlecruisers.

> **Coordinate trade routes** among members to maximize profits across different regions of the galaxy.

> A full 20-member corporation with diverse ship types can control entire regions of the galaxy.
