# Automation

Automation allows your ships to perform tasks automatically on a timer. It requires the **Basic Automation** technology (which itself requires Basic Crafting).

## Requirements

1. Research **Basic Crafting** technology (5,000 credits, 30 min)
2. Research **Basic Automation** technology (20,000 credits, 1 hour)
3. Have a ship with sufficient fuel

## Rules

- **Max active tasks per player**: 3
- **Execution interval**: Tasks execute every 60 seconds
- **Fuel multiplier**: Automated tasks consume **1.5×** normal fuel

## Task Types

### Trade Routes

Set up automated trade runs between two ports. Your ship will:
1. Buy commodities at the source port
2. Jump to the destination sector
3. Sell commodities at the destination port
4. Return and repeat

### Mining Runs

Automate resource extraction from planets with colonies. Your ship will:
1. Collect produced resources from a colony
2. Jump to a nearby port
3. Sell the resources
4. Return and repeat

## Tips

> **Fuel costs matter** — automated tasks use 1.5× fuel. Make sure your routes are profitable enough to cover the extra fuel expense.

> **Use efficient engines** — Higher fuel efficiency engines like the Quantum Drive (2.0×) or Hyperspace Drive (2.5×) make automation much more cost-effective.

> **Start with short routes** — Shorter trade routes consume less fuel per cycle and generate faster returns.
